Introduction

Kwilibri provides administrative and compliance services to South Africa organizations and individuals through a combination of in-house and managed third party services.

Services provided covers the entire lifecycle of companies. This includes startup, operating lifespan through to wrapping up of operations and deregistration or liquidation.

Services

Starting up a South African company

We assist prospective business owners in setting up new companies. This includes:

  • Selecting a suitable, available name
  • Registering a new company with the Companies and Intellectual Properties Commission (CIPC)
  • Registering the company with the South African Revenue Service (SARS)
  • Opening a bank account for the company
  • Registering a domain name for the company
  • Appointing a public officer/representative and register them with SARS
  • Registering the company as an Employer.
  • Obtaining any required trading licenses.
  • Issuing shares to shareholders.
  • Serving as registered office.

Operating a South African Company

We assist companies in operating their business. This includes:
  • Keeping financial records.
  • Performing payroll calculations and activities.
  • Submitting annual returns to regulatory bodies such as SARS and CIPC.
  • Making payments to suppliers and collecting payments from customers/clients.
  • Managing the company's assets.
  • Arranging shareholders meetings.
  • Administering debt and financing portfolios.
  • Digitizing, indexing, storing and securely disposing of documents.
  • Manage inbound and outbound mail and courier requirements.
